1. Recipe Name:
   Chocolate Milkshake

2. List of ingredients:
   - 2 cups of milk
   - 3 tablespoons of sugar
   - 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup
   - 1 cup of ice cream
   - Whipped cream (optional)
   - Chocolate shavings (optional)

3. Recipe steps:
   1. In a blender, combine the milk, sugar, chocolate syrup, and ice cream.
   2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
   3. Pour the milkshake into a glass.
   4. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings if desired.
   5. Serve cold and enjoy!

4. Cooking Time:
   5 minutes

5. Meal Preferences:
   Snack

Additional tip:
- You can customize this milkshake by adding in other ingredients like peanut butter, bananas, or even a shot of espresso for a different flavor twist.
